GALAXY GROUP is one of the Egyptian leading companies with an experience more than 30 years in providing industrial equipments, processes, and technology for different industries

GALAXY GROUP was founded by Eng. Mahfouz Darwish (Director) a pioneer in the industrial field. Thanks to his determination to provide the best industrial services and solutions and his clearly defined strategies in addition to his fruitful ongoing business network both nationally and internationally, he guided GALAXY GROUP to the shore of success.

GALAXY GROUP reputation is earned by our conduct: “what we say, what we do, the products we deliver, the services we provide, and the way we act and treat others” as this is the only way to do business!!

Our Purpose :

  • (Passion for Winning)

  • (Trust)

Our Principles :

  • (Externally – Suppliers Focused)
